BRIEF ABOUT US.

Houz is a creative team do architecture and interior

Our vision is not limited to bright and bold combinations but caters to every client’s dream space.


In the few years that we have been working, we have influenced the approach to design in the industry. We see each process as collaborations with not only homeowners but also entrepreneurs – which result in a fresh mix that redefine any space or place.


WHAT
WE
DO


Home Design

Houz has been offering clients timeless design solutions for more than 10 years. We strive to provide our clients with the latest in furnishings for the home, including innovative custom furnishings, window treatments, lighting, accessories and unique artwork by local artists.


Garden & outdoor

Your outdoor living space, including your garden should be an extension of your indoor living space. Think of it as another room in your home. At Houz, we offer many styles of outdoor furniture to choose from, in addition, there is a wide variety of outdoor lighting and rugs to complete the design.


Consultant

From initial consultation, to product selection, to final installation, Houz handles every detail. We are a true end-to-end design service. So you can be sure your “after” will be a dream come true. Since our meetings take place in your home or our office, we’ll work with you to help visualize a design solution that aligns with your taste, space, and budget. And, our initial consultation is complimentary!
